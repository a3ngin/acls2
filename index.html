ACLS Crash Course

According to the American Heart Association (2011), every year 350,000 people in the US and Canada have a cardiac arrest and receive some form of CPR and nearly half of them occur while they are in the hospital. Every second of hesitation or delay in treatment is literally a matter of life or death for these individuals.
